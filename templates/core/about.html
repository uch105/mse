About Materials Science Hub

We're building the world's most comprehensive platform for materials science research, education, and collaboration. Our mission is to accelerate innovation by connecting researchers, students, and professionals with cutting-edge tools and resources.

Our Mission

To democratize access to materials science knowledge and tools, enabling researchers, engineers, and students worldwide to accelerate innovation in materials development and application.

We believe that by providing comprehensive resources, AI-powered assistance, and a collaborative community, we can help solve some of the world's most pressing materials challenges.

Our Vision

To become the definitive global platform for materials science, where every researcher, student, and professional can access the tools, data, and community needed to push the boundaries of materials innovation.

We envision a future where materials discovery is accelerated through AI, collaboration, and open access to scientific knowledge.

Founder's Message

Tanvir Saklan

Founder

During my studies in the Department of Materials Science & Engineering, I faced several limitations while searching for good learning materials. Therefore, I decided to create a platform that would provide comprehensive resources for students and researchers in materials science. My goal is to make high-quality educational content accessible to everyone, fostering a global community of materials scientists and engineers.

- Tanvir Saklan
